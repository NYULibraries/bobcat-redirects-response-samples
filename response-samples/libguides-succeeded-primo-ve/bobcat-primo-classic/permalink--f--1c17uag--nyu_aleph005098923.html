Service Alert: Borrowing & Requesting Changes for Dec. 2023 and Jan. 2024

Due to a system migration, some services will be paused starting Dec. 8, 2023. For full details about the service changes visit our status page.

You may see changes to request options throughout December.

  • Dec. 8 - Last day to place EZBorrow Requests. After Dec. 8th request through Interlibrary Loan (ILL).
  • Dec. 18 - Last day to place a request for pickup and delivery, including from offsite.
  • Dec. 22 - Last day for all circulation services, including self-service from the stacks.

How do I get this item and how long will it take?

  • If you want a book now, and it is "Available", find the item on the shelf and check it out at the Circulation Desk. Use our call numbers and floor maps resource for help.
  • If you can wait 3-5 days, request the item for locker pick-up.
  • If you only need 1-2 chapters of a book, request a digital scan.

Provides an introduction to the social and cultural foundations of education. This area of education reflects many of the conflicts, tensions, and forces in American society, focusing on issues such as race, gender, socioeconomic class, the impact of technology on learning, what it means to be educated, and the role of teaching and learning in a societal context.
